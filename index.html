About Us

We are contemporary education consulting firm established in 2015 with a specialty in delivering educational services. The team consists of knowledgeable educational consultants, researchers, academic experts, service providers, and customer service staff. We have achieved significant milestones in academic research at various universities, including Taraba State University, Michael Okpara University, AE-FUNAI, EBSU, and Baze University. Our team comprises of experienced researchers from Nigeria, Ghana, South Africa, the US, the UK, and Australia, capable of conducting thorough research and providing detailed, plagiarism-free work. Our researchers are professionals in their respective fields and our team has a strong track record of delivering exceptional results and receiving recommendations from government agencies and NGOs in Northern Nigeria, such as DI Rescue House, IIFGD, SGDN, and ICGD. We have successfully secured substantial grant funding worth over 200,000 million naira for more than 40 NGOs and have effectively managed numerous governmental projects from inception to completion in 2017 and 2018.
